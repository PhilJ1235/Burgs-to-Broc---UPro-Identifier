Clear Foods

Use this tool to identify how processed the foods you eat are. We use the NOVA classification system which has 4 levels:

  • 1 - Unprocessed or minimally processed food
  • 2 - Processed Culinary Ingredients
  • 3 - Processed Food
  • 4 - Ultra-processed food
